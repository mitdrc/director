Intro to LCM
============

This tutorial introduces the basics of LCM.  LCM is the `Lightweight Communication
and Marshalling <https://lcm-proj.github.io/>`_ library.

The LCM documentation contains a `collection of tutorials <https://lcm-proj.github.io/tutorial_general.html>`_,
and in particular a `Python tutorial <https://lcm-proj.github.io/tut_python.html>`_.
